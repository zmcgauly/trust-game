If the proposer sends S points and the multiplier is M, the responder has S x M points available. If the responder sends back R points, the round points are:

Proposer: 20 - S + R

Responder: S x M - R

For example, if the proposer sends 10 points and the multiplier is 3, the responder has 30 points available. If the responder sends back 12 points, the proposer earns 20 - 10 + 12 = 22 points and the responder earns 30 - 12 = 18 points for that round. If that round is chosen for payout then they would respectively earn $22 and $18.
