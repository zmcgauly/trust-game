Welcome, and thank you for participating in this research study.

Please place all electronic devices out of reach for the duration of the experiment. To ensure that all participants have the same experimental environment, conversation and other forms of communication are not permitted while the session is in progress.

If you have a question at any time, please raise your hand. An experimenter will come to your workstation and answer your question privately.

You may review these instructions at any time during the experiment by selecting the Instructions button located in the upper-right corner of your screen.

This study investigates economic decision-making. Throughout the experiment, you will make decisions that determine both your own earnings and the earnings of another participant. Likewise, the decisions of other participants will affect their own earnings and, in some cases, yours.

For participating in today's session, you will receive a show-up payment of $[Show-Up Fee]. This payment is guaranteed and is separate from any earnings you accumulate during the experiment. You will receive the show-up payment regardless of the decisions made during the session.

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw from the experiment at any time without penalty. If you choose to leave before the experiment has concluded, you will still receive your show-up payment but will forfeit any additional earnings that could have been earned during the experiment.
