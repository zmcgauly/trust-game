Please put all electronic devices away for the duration of the experiment. Please remain quiet while the experiment is in session. If you have a question, raise your hand and an experimenter will meet with you directly. At any time if you wish to see the instructions again select the instructions button in the upper right-hand corner of your screen.

This is an experiment on economic decision making. The decisions you and others make determine the final amount of money you and other participants will earn. By participating, you have earned a show-up fee of $ (Varible). This amount is separate from your potential earnings throughout the experiment. Regardless of your decisions throughout the experiment, you will earn the show-up fee.

You are free to leave at any time regardless of the completion status of the experiment. Should you choose to leave before the experiment is complete, you will still be given your show-up fee but will forfeit any payoff from your decisions.
