There are proposers and responders. Roles stay fixed for the full session.

In each round, the proposer receives 20 points and chooses how many whole points to send to the responder. The sent points are tripled.

The responder then chooses how many of the tripled points to send back. There are two rounds with each partner. After each period, partners rotate.
