Instructions Summary

Your assigned role is either proposer or responder and remains the same for the entire session.

The experiment begins with two practice rounds that are not eligible for payment. After the practice rounds, there are five periods with two decision rounds in each period.

At the beginning of every period, you are matched with a participant who has the opposite role. You remain paired with that participant for both rounds in the period.

In each round, the proposer receives 20 points and chooses how many whole points, from 0 to 20, to send to the responder. The points sent are multiplied by either 3 or 6.

The proposer does not know whether the multiplier will be 3 or 6 when making the sending decision. After multiplication, the responder observes the points sent, the multiplier used, and the total points available.

Round earnings are:

\[ \text{proposer earnings} = 20 - \text{points sent by proposer} + \text{points returned by responder} \]

\[ \text{responder earnings} = \text{points available to responder} - \text{points returned by responder} \]

At the end of the experiment, you will answer a brief questionnaire about yourself and the participants with whom you were matched.
