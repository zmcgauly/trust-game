Instructions Summary

Your role is either proposer or responder and stays the same for the entire session. The session begins with two practice rounds that cannot be selected for payoff.

After the practice rounds, there are 5 periods. In each period, you are matched with one partner for 2 rounds. After each period, partners rotate.

In each round, the proposer receives 20 points and chooses how many whole points, from 0 to 20, to send to the responder.

Points sent by the proposer are multiplied before the responder decides how much to send back. The responder sees how many points were sent and the total points available after multiplication.

If the proposer sends S points, the multiplier is M, and the responder sends back R points, round points are:

  • Proposer: 20 - S + R
  • Responder: S x M - R

At the end of the game, you will answer demographic questions about yourself and questions about the people you were matched with.
