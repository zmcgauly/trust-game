Participants are assigned one of two roles:

  • Proposer
  • Responder

Your assigned role will remain the same for the entire session.

The experiment begins with two practice rounds. These practice rounds are provided solely to familiarize you with the decision-making process and interface. They will not be eligible for payment.

Following the practice rounds, the experiment consists of five periods.

Each period contains two decision rounds. At the beginning of every period, you will be randomly matched with a new participant who has the opposite role from your own. You will remain paired with that participant for both rounds within the period before being assigned a new partner in the next period.
