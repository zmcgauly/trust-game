This study has two roles: proposers and responders. Your role will stay the same for the entire session.

The session begins with two practice rounds. Practice rounds are only for learning the rules and cannot be selected for payoff. After the practice rounds, there are 5 periods. In each period, you are matched with one partner for 2 rounds. After each period, partners rotate. Your partner will be someone with the opposite role to you.

In some periods, you may see your partner's picture. In other periods, your partner may be shown as anonymous. Your own picture is shown on decision screens so you can confirm which participant you are.
