After you and your partner have concluded your decisions, you will be provided with a screen to review the rounds' results including how much was sent, received, and your final payoffs.

If you were assigned the role of the proposer, you will then be asked questions regarding what you believe the multiplier for that round was. To answer you will stack the balls on the two multiplier options. You will have ten balls to place across the two options. If you are confident in one, you may place all ten on one. You may also split them up according to what you believe and how confident you are in that belief.

For every ball placed correctly you will receive an additional $1 if that round is selected. See the visual below for a demonstration of what it could look like.

If at anytime you would like to access your round review while answering this question, simply hover your mouse over the bolded and underlined title round review and a box will pop up with the results.
